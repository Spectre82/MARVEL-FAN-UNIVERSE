Hulk (Earth-616)

Avengers Heroes Earth-616

"Hulk smash!"

[The Incredible Hulk]

Bruce Banner, the Incredible Hulk, is one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe. When angered, Banner transforms into the Hulk, a green-skinned behemoth with nearly limitless strength.

Despite his destructive tendencies, Hulk has become a valued member of the Avengers and a force for good. His struggle to control his alter ego and maintain his humanity has made him one of the most compelling characters in superhero fiction.
