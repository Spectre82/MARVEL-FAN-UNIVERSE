Jean Grey (Earth-616)

X-Men Heroes Earth-616

"The Phoenix is reborn from the ashes."

[X-Men]

Jean Grey is one of the original X-Men and one of the most powerful telepaths in the Marvel Universe. She possesses both telepathic and telekinetic abilities, making her an invaluable member of the team.

Jean is also the host of the Phoenix Force, an ancient and powerful cosmic entity that grants her god-like powers. Her journey with the Phoenix Force has made her one of the most complex and influential characters in the X-Men's history.
