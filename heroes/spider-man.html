Spider-Man (Earth-616)

Avengers Heroes Spider-Verse Earth-616

"With great power comes great responsibility."

[Amazing Fantasy #15]

Peter Parker, better known as Spider-Man, gained his powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Initially using his abilities for personal gain, a tragedy involving his Uncle Ben taught him that with great power comes great responsibility. Since then, he has dedicated his life to protecting New York City and its citizens, while balancing his superhero duties with his personal life and career.

Despite facing numerous challenges and personal losses, Spider-Man remains one of Marvel's most relatable heroes. His quick wit, scientific genius, and unwavering sense of responsibility make him a beloved figure in the superhero community. Whether working solo or alongside the Avengers, Spider-Man's dedication to doing what's right, even at great personal cost, has inspired generations of heroes and readers alike.
