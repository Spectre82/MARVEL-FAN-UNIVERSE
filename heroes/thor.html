Thor (Earth-616)

Asgardians Avengers Heroes Earth-616

"Whosoever holds this hammer, if he be worthy, shall possess the power of Thor."

[Thor (2011)]

Thor Odinson, the God of Thunder and former crown prince of Asgard, is one of the most powerful beings in the Marvel Universe. As the son of Odin and brother to Loki, Thor has protected both Earth and Asgard for centuries. His journey from an arrogant prince to a humble hero and eventually the King of Asgard showcases his growth as both a warrior and a leader.

Wielding the enchanted hammer Mjolnir, Thor possesses incredible powers including control over lightning and storms, superhuman strength, and the ability to fly. His time with the Avengers has made him one of Earth's mightiest defenders, while his experiences have taught him valuable lessons about humility, sacrifice, and the true meaning of worthiness. Despite his godly status, Thor's connection to humanity and his dedication to protecting both realms have made him a beloved figure among heroes and mortals alike.
