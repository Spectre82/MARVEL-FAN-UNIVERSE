Doctor Strange (Earth-616)

Masters of the Mystic Arts Heroes Sorcerer Supreme Earth-616

"The possibilities are endless."

[Doctor Strange (2016)]

Doctor Stephen Strange was once a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon whose career was shattered after a devastating car accident damaged his hands. Seeking healing, he discovered the mystic arts under the Ancient One's tutelage and became Earth's primary protector against magical and mystical threats. As the Sorcerer Supreme, he guards our reality from interdimensional threats and dark forces that seek to destroy it.

Master of the mystic arts and wielder of powerful magical artifacts like the Eye of Agamotto, Doctor Strange combines his medical knowledge with supernatural abilities to defend Earth from mystical threats. His journey from a self-centered surgeon to a selfless protector of reality showcases his remarkable character development and dedication to safeguarding our dimension.
