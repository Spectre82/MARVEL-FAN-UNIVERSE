Thanos (Earth-616)

Antagonist Villains Earth-616

"I am inevitable."

[The Avengers: Infinity War]

Thanos, known as the Mad Titan, is one of the most powerful villains in the Marvel Universe. His quest for universal balance through destruction has made him a formidable threat to heroes across the cosmos.

As a powerful Eternal, Thanos possesses incredible physical abilities and advanced technology. His obsession with the Infinity Stones and his love for Death have made him one of the most iconic and terrifying villains in comic book history.
