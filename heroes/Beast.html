Beast (Earth-616)

X-Men Heroes Earth-616

"Science is the key to our future."

[X-Men]

Dr. Henry "Hank" McCoy, known as Beast, is one of the original X-Men and a brilliant scientist. His mutation has given him enhanced physical abilities and a feline appearance, but his intellect remains one of his greatest assets.

As both a scientist and a superhero, Beast has made significant contributions to mutant science and has been a valuable member of the X-Men for decades. His combination of physical strength and scientific expertise makes him a unique and powerful hero.
