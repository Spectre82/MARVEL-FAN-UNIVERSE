Star-Lord (Earth-616)

Guardians of the Galaxy Heroes Earth-616

"I am Star-Lord."

[Guardians of the Galaxy]

Peter Quill, known as Star-Lord, is a space pirate turned hero and the leader of the Guardians of the Galaxy. His quick wit, combat skills, and leadership abilities make him a formidable force in the cosmic arena.

Star-Lord's ability to bring together a diverse group of misfits and turn them into a powerful team has made him one of the most influential figures in the Marvel Universe's cosmic sector.
