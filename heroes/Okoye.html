Okoye (Earth-616)

Wakandans Heroes Earth-616

"I am the spearhead of the Dora Milaje."

[Black Panther (2018)]

Okoye is the general of the Dora Milaje, Wakanda's elite all-female special forces unit. Known for her unwavering loyalty to Wakanda and her exceptional combat skills, Okoye is one of the most formidable warriors in the Marvel Universe.

As a member of the Dora Milaje, Okoye has trained extensively in various combat styles and tactics. Her leadership and combat prowess make her a respected figure both in Wakanda and among Earth's heroes.
