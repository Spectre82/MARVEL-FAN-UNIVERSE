Wolverine (Earth-616)

X-Men Heroes Earth-616

"I'm the best at what I do, but what I do best isn't very nice."

[Wolverine]

Wolverine, also known as Logan, is one of the most iconic X-Men. His healing factor and adamantium-laced skeleton make him nearly indestructible, while his enhanced senses and combat skills make him a formidable warrior.

Despite his rough exterior and troubled past, Wolverine has become a trusted member of the X-Men and a mentor to many younger mutants. His complex personality and tragic history have made him one of the most beloved characters in the Marvel Universe.
