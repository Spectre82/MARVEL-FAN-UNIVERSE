Loki (Earth-616)

Asgardians Anti-Hero Magic Users Earth-616

"I am burdened with glorious purpose."

[The Avengers (2012)]

Loki, the God of Mischief and adopted son of Odin, is one of the most complex characters in the Marvel Universe. Born a Frost Giant but raised as an Asgardian prince, his complicated relationship with his adoptive family, particularly his brother Thor, has driven many of his actions throughout his existence. A master of magic and deception, Loki's journey has taken him from villain to anti-hero, and sometimes even hero.

While initially known for his villainous schemes and betrayals, Loki has evolved significantly over time. His intelligence, magical abilities, and cunning make him a formidable force in any situation. Despite his mischievous nature, he has occasionally allied with heroes to face greater threats, showing that even the God of Mischief can choose to do what's right when it truly matters.
