Shuri (Earth-616)

Wakandans Heroes Earth-616

"The future is mine to create."

[Black Panther (2018)]

Shuri, the younger sister of T'Challa, is a genius inventor and warrior who serves as Wakanda's chief scientist. Her innovations have revolutionized Wakandan technology, making her one of the most brilliant minds in the Marvel Universe.

Shuri is also a skilled combatant and has taken on the mantle of Black Panther in her brother's absence. Her combination of technological prowess and combat skills makes her a formidable hero in her own right.
