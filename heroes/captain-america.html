Captain America (Earth-616)

Avengers Heroes Super-Soldier Earth-616

"I can do this all day."

[Captain America: The First Avenger (2011)]

Steve Rogers, known as Captain America, is a World War II veteran and the world's first super-soldier. After volunteering for a secret government program, he was transformed from a frail young man into the peak of human potential. Armed with his iconic shield and unwavering moral compass, he leads the Avengers in protecting Earth from various threats.

Despite being a man out of time, having been frozen in ice for decades, Captain America has adapted to the modern world while maintaining his core values of honor, duty, and sacrifice. His leadership abilities and tactical genius make him one of the most respected heroes in the Marvel Universe, and his unwavering dedication to justice has inspired generations of heroes.
