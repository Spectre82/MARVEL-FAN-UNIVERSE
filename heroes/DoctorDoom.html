Doctor Doom (Earth-616)

Antagonist Villains Earth-616

"I am Doom, and this is my world."

[Doctor Doom]

Doctor Victor von Doom, ruler of Latveria, is one of the most dangerous and brilliant villains in the Marvel Universe. His genius-level intellect and mastery of science and magic make him a formidable opponent for Earth's heroes.

Doom's tragic past and twisted sense of morality have made him one of the most complex and memorable villains in comic book history. His combination of technological prowess and mystical abilities makes him a constant threat to the Marvel Universe.
