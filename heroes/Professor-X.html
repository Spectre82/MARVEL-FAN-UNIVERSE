Professor X (Earth-616)

X-Men Heroes Earth-616

"The world is a better place when we work together."

[X-Men]

Professor Charles Xavier, also known as Professor X, is a powerful telepath and mutant rights activist. He founded the X-Men to promote peaceful coexistence between humans and mutants, using his vast mental powers to protect both species.

Professor X's telepathic abilities make him one of the most powerful mutants in the Marvel Universe. His leadership and vision have shaped the X-Men into one of the most influential superhero teams.
