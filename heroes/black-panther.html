Black Panther (Earth-616)

Wakandans Heroes Avengers Earth-616

"A king protects his people, at any cost."

[Black Panther (2018)]

T'Challa, the Black Panther, is the king and protector of Wakanda, a technologically advanced African nation hidden from the world. After succeeding his father, T'Challa took on the mantle of Black Panther, using his enhanced abilities and advanced technology to defend his nation and the world.

As Black Panther, T'Challa possesses superhuman abilities granted by the Heart-Shaped Herb, making him one of the most formidable heroes in the Marvel Universe. His intelligence, combat skills, and leadership make him a respected figure among Earth's Mightiest Heroes.
