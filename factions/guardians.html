Guardians of the Galaxy

Defenders of the Cosmos

A ragtag team of intergalactic misfits who have joined forces to protect the galaxy from cosmic threats. Led by Peter Quill, aka Star-Lord, the Guardians travel across space, taking on missions that often put them at odds with powerful entities while maintaining their unique brand of chaotic heroism and family-like bond.
