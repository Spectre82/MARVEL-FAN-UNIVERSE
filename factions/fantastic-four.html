Fantastic Four

Marvel's First Family

The Fantastic Four gained their powers after being exposed to cosmic rays during a scientific mission to space. Led by Reed Richards, they use their abilities to protect Earth from various threats while continuing their scientific research and exploration. Their unique dynamic as both a superhero team and a family sets them apart in the Marvel Universe.
