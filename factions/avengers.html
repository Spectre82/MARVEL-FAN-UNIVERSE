The Avengers

Earth's Mightiest Heroes

The Avengers are Earth's premier team of enhanced individuals, dedicated to protecting the planet from threats too great for any single hero to handle. Founded by Nick Fury and initially comprised of Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, the team has grown to include numerous heroes over the years.
