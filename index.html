Venom War

The symbiote invasion begins as Venom leads the charge against a new threat...

Venom War: The Symbiote Apocalypse

Release Date: March 2025

Event Overview

When a mysterious cosmic entity awakens ancient symbiotes across the universe, Eddie Brock and his son Dylan must unite Earth's heroes to prevent a symbiote apocalypse.

Key Plot Points
  • Ancient symbiote god Knull's dormant seeds begin awakening across the galaxy
  • Dylan Brock discovers he can control all symbiotes
  • The Avengers and X-Men must work with Venom to prevent Earth's infection
  • Flash Thompson returns as Anti-Venom with crucial information
Major Players
  • Eddie Brock (Venom)
  • Dylan Brock
  • Flash Thompson (Anti-Venom)
  • Carnage
  • The Life Foundation Symbiotes

Doom's Reign

Doctor Doom finally achieves his ultimate goal, but at what cost?

Doom's Reign: The Age of Latveria

Release Date: April 2025

Event Overview

Victor von Doom discovers a way to harness the power of multiple cosmic entities, establishing a new world order with Latveria at its center. As reality begins to warp under his rule, heroes must decide whether to resist or adapt to Doom's perfect world.

Key Plot Points
  • Doom combines the Power Cosmic, Phoenix Force, and mystical artifacts
  • Reality warps as Latveria becomes the center of human civilization
  • The Fantastic Four discover a resistance movement from an alternate timeline
  • Several heroes choose to serve Doom, believing his world is better
Major Players
  • Doctor Doom
  • Reed Richards
  • Doctor Strange
  • Namor
  • The Resistance (led by Sue Storm)

X-Men: Evolution

A new era begins for mutantkind as evolution takes an unexpected turn...

X-Men: Evolution - The Next Step

Release Date: May 2025

Event Overview

A mysterious phenomenon causes mutants worldwide to undergo a secondary mutation, granting them enhanced abilities but at a terrible cost. As the X-Men race to understand this evolution, they discover a shocking truth about mutation's ultimate purpose.

Key Plot Points
  • Global secondary mutation crisis affects all mutants
  • Discovery of an ancient mutant civilization
  • Jean Grey establishes a psychic network to help control new powers
  • Revelation about humanity's evolutionary destiny
Major Players
  • Charles Xavier
  • Jean Grey
  • Magneto
  • The Evolutionary
  • The First Mutant (new character)

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Marvel and our community

What is the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU)?

+

The Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) is a groundbreaking interconnected series of superhero films and TV shows based on Marvel Comics characters. Key aspects include:

  • Launched in 2008 with Iron Man, now spanning multiple phases
  • Features a shared universe where events of one film affect others
  • Includes major franchises like Avengers, Thor, and Spider-Man
  • Currently expanding into the multiverse with Phase 4 and beyond
  • Has grossed over $22 billion worldwide, making it the highest-grossing film franchise

How do I start reading Marvel Comics?

+

Getting started with Marvel Comics is easier than you might think:

  • Digital Platforms:
    • Marvel Unlimited: Subscription service with 27,000+ comics
    • Comixology: Digital store for individual purchases
    • Marvel Comics App: Official app for mobile reading
  • Recommended Starting Points:
    • Ultimate Spider-Man: Modern retelling of Spider-Man's origin
    • Avengers: Earth's Mightiest Heroes
    • House of M: Popular event series
    • Civil War: Major crossover event

What's the difference between Marvel Comics and MCU?

+

While both share characters and stories, there are key differences:

  • Comics have a longer history, starting in 1939
  • MCU adapts and simplifies comic storylines for film
  • Comics feature more complex narratives and character relationships
  • MCU maintains a single timeline, while comics have multiple universes
  • Comics introduce new characters and storylines that may later appear in the MCU

Who are the main Marvel superhero teams?

+

Marvel features several iconic superhero teams, each with unique characteristics:

  • The Avengers: Earth's mightiest heroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, and Thor
  • X-Men: Mutant heroes fighting for equality and survival
  • Fantastic Four: Marvel's first family of superheroes
  • Guardians of the Galaxy: Cosmic heroes
  • Defenders: Street-level heroes protecting their neighborhoods
